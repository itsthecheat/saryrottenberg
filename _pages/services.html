Services

Individuals

Our Integrative approach drawing from psychodynamic, cognitive behavioral, strengths-based and mindfulness practices, tailoring to and honoring your unique needs.

Relationship Counseling

The special set of challenges that comes with romantic relationships are addressed bringing to light patterns of communications, and needs that are asked to be met.

Family Counseling

All family formations are welcome to counseling, monogamous, non traditional, single parenthood and co-parenting.

Group Counseling

Working in a group with others can be supportive, invigorating, and be a space for seeing and experiencing that others are challenged in similar, yet different ways. Contact us for current group offerings.
